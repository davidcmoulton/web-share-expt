Web share target

Allows me, when I'm installed as a progressive web app, to be the target of a share i.e. to appear in your device's sharing menu.

Tap the Add to Home screen prompt when it appears. If it doesn't appear after a few seconds, I'm either installed already, or your device / browser combo doesn't support PWAs at the moment. Once installed, I should become available in your device's sharing menu.

Once I'm installed, return to the main page for the next step.
